ABOUT COMPANY

Poyp00l was founded in 2010 by a young fashion designer with a vision to create elegant and chic fashion for women. Starting with a small store, Poyp00l has grown to become a leading fashion brand in Indonesia with over 20 stores nationwide. Our journey began with a passion for fashion and a commitment to quality, and we have since become known for our stylish and sophisticated designs. Today, Poyp00l is a beloved brand among women in Indonesia, and we continue to strive for excellence in everything we do.

VISION & MISSION

Vision

To become a leading fashion brand in Indonesia known for its quality, design, and excellent service.

Mission

  1. To provide high-quality fashion and stylish designs for Indonesian women.
  2. To deliver good and satisfying service to our customers.
  3. To increase awareness and appreciation of elegant and chic fashion in Indonesia.
  4. 4. To develop a professional and dedicated team to achieve the company's goals.
